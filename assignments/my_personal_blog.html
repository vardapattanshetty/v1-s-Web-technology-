Mysore Blog

Posted by Varsha on November 5, 2020

Mysore, known as the cultural capital of Karnataka, is a city rich in history and tradition. From the grandeur of Mysore Palace to the peaceful ambiance of Chamundi Hill, every corner of the city tells a story. One of the highlights of my trip was exploring the bustling Devaraja Market, where the colors and scents of flowers, fruits, and spices create a vibrant tapestry. The Mysore Zoo is also a must-visit, especially for families. A trip to Mysore is incomplete without savoring the famous Mysore Pak, a traditional sweet that melts in your mouth.

Delhi Blog

Posted by Varda on September 20, 2022

Delhi, the heart of India, is a city that effortlessly blends the old with the new. The historical landmarks like the Red Fort, India Gate, and Qutub Minar stand as proud reminders of the city's rich past. The bustling streets of Chandni Chowk, with their myriad of shops and food stalls, offer a sensory overload. On the other hand, modern attractions like the Lotus Temple and Akshardham Temple showcase the city's contemporary architecture. Delhi's culinary scene is a food lover's paradise, with everything from street food delights to fine dining experiences.

Goa Blog

Posted by Arjun on March 15, 2021

Goa, the ultimate beach destination, is a paradise for sun-seekers and adventure enthusiasts. With its golden sandy beaches, clear blue waters, and vibrant nightlife, Goa offers something for everyone. One of my favorite spots was Anjuna Beach, famous for its flea market and lively beach parties. For a quieter experience, Palolem Beach is perfect with its serene environment. Besides the beaches, the Portuguese influence is evident in the architecture, particularly in Old Goa with its beautiful churches. Don't miss the opportunity to indulge in Goan cuisine, especially seafood dishes like prawn curry and fish recheado.
